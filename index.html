PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Inkjet printing technology uses miniscule, liquid ink droplets that are propelled onto paper or other substrates during the printing process, where they rapidly dry to produce high-quality, high-resolution colorful text and images.

Dye-based inks are less expensive. They are able to produce more vibrant colors when printing as the ink is absorbed into the fiber of the material. Dye-based inks are more reslient when printing. The ink sits on the surface of the material it is printed on and fuses with the fibers. Pigment-based ink is ideal for printing durable labels, such as GHS and BS 5609 compliant labels.

Inkjet printing supports roll and fanfold labels, as well as tags. Roll labels must have 3" core innter diameter. Perforated or non-perforated labels can be used as well as continuous labels. Many inkjet printers support labels with glossy or matte finished for applications that requre different vibrancy or reflectivity levels. We carry various label sizes in different materials and colors to ensure you are choosing the best label for your application.
